Eggplant Parmersian Recipe

Ingredients Needed

  • 3 Large Eggplants
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 4 cups of Italian Breadcrumbs
  • 6 cups of spaghetti sauce
  • 16 ounces mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of parmesian cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried basil

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F
  2. Dip the eggplant slices into eggwash and then into bread crumbs. Then place the eggplant on sheet
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 5 minutes then flip it over and bake for another 5 minutes
  4. Spread spaghetti sauce all over the bottom of the tray
  5. Place a layer of eggplants on top of the sauce
  6. Sprinkle both parmesian and mozzarella cheese on top of the eggplant
  7. Repeat the last 3 steps until you fully reach the top of the tray
  8. Sprinkle some dry Oregano on top
  9. Place the try in the 350F oven for 35 minutes
